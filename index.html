Profile of the Registrar (Academic Research and Outreach)

Profile Summary

Dr. Meshack Mwandoe Pongah is a Doctor of Philosophy in Human Resource Management from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. He has a Bachelor of Education Arts from Egerton University and MBA, Human Resource Management from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. Dr. Mwandoe has also received Diploma and Advanced Diploma training in Human Resource Management from Institute of Commercial Management, U.K between 2001 and 2002 respectively. He joined Taita Taveta University in 2008 as a Senior Administrative Assistant (Academic Research and Outreach). He has worked with the University to date and currently he is the Acting Registrar (Academic Research and Outreach).

Dr. Maurice Juma Ogada
